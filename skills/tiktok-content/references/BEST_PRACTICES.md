# TikTok Best Practices

Essential guidelines for TikTok success.

## Account Setup

### Profile Optimization
- **Username:** Easy to remember, consistent with other platforms
- **Profile Picture:** Clear, recognizable, on-brand
- **Bio:** Who you are + what you do + why they should follow
- **Link:** Use link-in-bio tools (Linktree, Beacons, Stan Store) for multiple links
- **Pinned Videos:** Your 3 best-performing or most representative videos

### Switch to Business/Creator Account
- Access to analytics
- Post scheduling (Business only)
- Creator marketplace eligibility
- Better insights into audience

## Content Creation

### Video Quality
- **Resolution:** Minimum 1080x1920 (vertical 9:16)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps minimum, 60fps preferred
- **Lighting:** Natural light or ring light for face videos
- **Audio:** Clear sound, trending audio when appropriate
- **Stability:** Use tripod or gimbal for smooth footage

### Text and Captions
- Use TikTok's native text tool (better for algorithm)
- Keep text large enough to read on mobile
- Use high contrast colors
- Don't cover your face with text
- Add captions for accessibility

### Thumbnails
- TikTok auto-generates thumbnails
- You can select a custom frame before posting
- Choose a frame with clear facial expression or result
- Avoid blurry or dark frames

## The Algorithm

### How the Algorithm Works
1. **Initial Test:** Video shown to small audience (100-500 viewers)
2. **Engagement Check:** Likes, comments, shares, watch time measured
3. **Expansion:** If engagement is good, shown to larger audience
4. **Viral Potential:** High engagement = exponential reach

### Key Ranking Factors
1. **Watch Time** (most important)
   - Aim for 80%+ average watch time
   - Hook viewers in first 3 seconds
   - Keep content engaging throughout

2. **Engagement Rate**
   - Likes, comments, shares, saves
   - Comments weighted higher than likes
   - Shares are highest value

3. **Rewatches**
   - Loop-worthy content
   - Fast-paced editing
   - Information that needs processing

4. **Completion Rate**
   - How many watch to the end
   - Series content helps (need to see part 2)

## Posting Strategy

### Best Times to Post (General)
- **Tuesday:** 7:00 AM, 11:00 AM
- **Wednesday:** 7:00 AM, 11:00 AM
- **Thursday:** 9:00 AM, 12:00 PM
- **Friday:** 9:00 AM, 5:00 PM

*Note: Check your analytics for YOUR best times*

### Posting Frequency
- **Growth Phase:** 1-3x daily
- **Maintenance:** 5-7x weekly
- **Minimum:** 3x weekly to stay relevant

### Content Mix
- 40% Educational/Value
- 30% Entertainment/Trends
- 20% Behind the Scenes/Personal
- 10% Promotional

## Growth Strategies

### The Follow/Unfollow Myth
- Don't do it - it's against ToS
- Creates low-quality followers
- Hurts engagement rate
- Can get account shadowbanned

### Effective Growth Tactics
1. **Trend Jacking**
   - Jump on trends early
   - Add unique twist for your niche
   - Use trending audio within 24-48 hours

2. **Series Content**
   - Create multi-part videos
   - "Part 1" drives follows for "Part 2"
   - Builds anticipation

3. **Comment Strategy**
   - Reply to every comment (first hour crucial)
   - Ask questions in your caption
   - Pin engaging comments
   - Comment on larger creators' videos

4. **Collaborations**
   - Duet/stitch with similar-sized creators
   - Cross-promote on other platforms
   - Participate in challenges together

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Content Mistakes
- ❌ Posting horizontal videos
- ❌ Using watermarks from other apps
- ❌ Ignoring comments
- ❌ Inconsistent posting
- ❌ Copying content without adding value
- ❌ Over-promoting products

### Technical Mistakes
- ❌ Poor lighting or audio
- ❌ Videos too long without value
- ❌ Not using captions
- ❌ Posting at random times
- ❌ Ignoring analytics

### Strategy Mistakes
- ❌ Buying followers or engagement
- ❌ Posting and ghosting
- ❌ Not engaging with community
- ❌ Giving up too early (takes 30-90 days minimum)
- ❌ Copying others without originality

## Analytics to Track

### Key Metrics
1. **Video Views** - Overall reach
2. **Profile Views** - Interest in your brand
3. **Follower Growth** - Account growth rate
4. **Engagement Rate** - (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Views
5. **Average Watch Time** - Content quality indicator
6. **Traffic Sources** - Where views come from

### Monthly Review Checklist
- [ ] Top performing videos (what made them work?)
- [ ] Worst performing videos (what to avoid?)
- [ ] Follower demographics (still your target audience?)
- [ ] Best posting times (adjusted for your audience)
- [ ] Content themes that performed well
- [ ] Engagement rate trend (improving or declining?)

## Monetization Pathways

### Creator Fund
- 10,000+ followers required
- 100,000+ video views in last 30 days
- Must be 18+
- Available in select countries

### Brand Partnerships
- Start reaching out at 10k followers
- Create media kit at 50k+
- Join creator marketplaces
- Maintain authentic brand alignment

### Live Gifts
- 1,000+ followers to go live
- Viewers send virtual gifts
- Convert to diamonds, then cash
- Engage heavily during lives

### Affiliate Marketing
- Share products with affiliate links
- Disclose partnerships (#ad, #sponsored)
- Only promote products you believe in
- Track performance per product

## Safety and Guidelines

### Community Guidelines
- No dangerous acts or challenges
- No hate speech or harassment
- No misinformation
- No sexual content or nudity
- No graphic violence

### Shadowban Signs
- Sudden drop in views
- Videos not appearing on FYP
- Hashtag searches don't show your content
- Only followers see your content

### If Shadowbanned
- Stop any suspicious activity
- Post consistently high-quality content
- Engage authentically
- Wait 1-2 weeks (usually temporary)
- Contact TikTok support if persists

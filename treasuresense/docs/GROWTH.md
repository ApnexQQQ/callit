# TreasureSense Growth Strategy

## North Star Metric
**Weekly Active Discoverers (WAD)** - Users who post at least one discovery per week

## Viral Loops

### 1. Share Certificate Loop
**Trigger:** User posts a discovery
**Action:** Generate beautiful digital certificate
**Share:** One-tap share to Instagram, TikTok, Twitter
**Hook:** "I found treasure! 🏆"
**Conversion:** Viewers download app to get their own certificate

**Implementation:**
- Auto-generate shareable image with discovery photo + map + certificate hash
- Pre-written captions with hashtags
- Deep link to app store
- "Find your own treasure" CTA

### 2. Zone Invite Loop
**Trigger:** User finds high-probability zone
**Action:** Invite friends to hunt together
**Hook:** "78% probability zone near you!"
**Conversion:** Friends join for group hunt

**Implementation:**
- "Hunt with Friends" button on zone detail
- Send SMS/ WhatsApp with zone location
- Group hunting bonuses (+25% XP)
- Live location sharing during hunt

### 3. Leaderboard Challenge Loop
**Trigger:** User ranks up or beats a friend's score
**Action:** Auto-share achievement
**Hook:** "I just passed you on the leaderboard! 🏅"
**Conversion:** Friends re-engage to reclaim rank

**Implementation:**
- Weekly leaderboard resets
- Push notifications when overtaken
- "Reclaim your spot" deep link
- Brag cards for social sharing

### 4. Discovery Reaction Loop
**Trigger:** Someone likes/comments on your discovery
**Action:** Notification + dopamine hit
**Hook:** "Your discovery is trending!"
**Conversion:** User posts more, engages with community

**Implementation:**
- Real-time push notifications
- "Trending" badge for popular posts
- Top discoverer spotlight
- Comment threading for discussions

## Growth Experiments

### Experiment 1: TikTok Integration
**Hypothesis:** Native TikTok sharing increases viral growth
**Test:** Add "Post to TikTok" button vs manual share
**Metric:** Shares per user, viral installs
**Duration:** 2 weeks

### Experiment 2: Group Hunts
**Hypothesis:** Group hunting increases retention
**Test:** Enable 4-person hunting parties with bonuses
**Metric:** D7 retention for group hunters vs solo
**Duration:** 3 weeks

### Experiment 3: AI Personalization
**Hypothesis:** Personalized zone recommendations increase engagement
**Test:** ML-based zone ranking vs distance-based
**Metric:** Zone visit rate, session duration
**Duration:** 2 weeks

### Experiment 4: Premium Teaser
